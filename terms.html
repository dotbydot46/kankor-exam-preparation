Terms

KEP is for learning support and exam preparation practice.

These terms are a starter draft for public launch. They should be reviewed before KEP becomes a real public product.

Important note

KEP does not guarantee admission, official results, or exam success. It helps students practise and prepare.

Educational use

KEP provides practice questions, mock exams, resources, study plans, and progress tools for learning support.

No official authority

KEP is not an official Kankor authority and should not be treated as an official result or admission system.

Content review

KEP tries to review content before publishing, but users should report mistakes so content can improve.

Volunteer content

Volunteer submissions may be edited, reviewed, accepted, rejected, or improved before publishing.

AI suggestions

AI may assist reviewers, but AI output should not be published automatically without human review.

Student responsibility

Students should use KEP as one study tool alongside books, teachers, official guidance, and consistent revision.

KEP

Kankor Exam Preparation support platform — built for practice, progress, and safer self-study.
