Final checks before sharing KEP publicly.

Launch rule

Do not launch to many students until login, practice, mock, progress, content quality, privacy, and contact pages are tested.

Public

Public website

  • Home page is clear.
  • About page explains mission.
  • Privacy and Terms pages exist.
  • Contact page has final safe contact details.
  • No confusing admin/setup links for students.
Student

Student flow

  • Signup/login works.
  • Practice loads questions.
  • Mock exam works.
  • Progress page loads attempts.
  • Study plan and library are easy to find.
Admin

Admin & content

  • Content Quality dashboard is checked.
  • Review DB can publish/reject.
  • AI suggestions remain reviewer-only.
  • At least one reviewer/admin account is ready.
  • Demo content is labelled if still used.
Security

Safety

  • No service-role or secret AI key is in frontend.
  • Supabase RLS policies are enabled.
  • Auth redirect URL is configured for live domain.
  • Privacy wording is visible.
  • Students are not asked for sensitive documents.
