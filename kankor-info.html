Kankor Info Center

Simple guidance for the full Kankor journey: what the exam is, how students prepare, how to avoid mistakes, and how to choose a field with purpose.

What is Kankor?

Kankor is Afghanistan’s national university entrance exam. KEP explains it in simple language and connects preparation with books, past papers, MCQs, mock exams, and field guidance.

Who is KEP for?

12th-grade students, rural students, students who cannot afford private courses, students with weak foundations, Afghan students abroad, and anyone preparing from home.

Important

Common mistakes students should avoid.

This area will later be updated from verified official sources each year.

✍️

Wrong details

Wrong spelling, Tazkira details, father’s name, or school information can create problems.

📷

Poor photo

Students need to understand photo rules and form requirements before submission.

🎯

Unclear field choice

Choosing fields without understanding subjects and goals can waste a student’s chance.
