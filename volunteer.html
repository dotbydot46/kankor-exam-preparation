Volunteer roles

Different people can help KEP in different ways.

Some volunteers can write questions. Some can review. Some can translate. Some can collect past papers or test the platform.

✍️

Question contributors

Create curriculum-based MCQs with options, correct answer, explanation, difficulty, and source.

✅

Teacher reviewers

Check if answers are correct, explanations are clear, and the question matches the subject.

📄

Past paper collectors

Help find official papers, centre papers, teacher-shared sheets, and mark source quality clearly.

🌐

Translators

Translate key content into Pashto, Dari, and English in simple student-friendly language.

🌱

Girls Education support

Help create gentle self-study plans, foundation packs, and private learning resources for girls.

📱

Student testers

Use the website and tell us what is confusing, slow, incorrect, or useful for real students.

Trust system

No random content should go straight to students.

KEP should protect trust by using source labels and a review process before publishing questions, books, and past papers.

01

Collect

Receive questions, books, notes, or papers with source details.

02

Check source

Label as official, textbook-based, centre paper, teacher-shared, or unverified.

03

Review

A reviewer checks the answer, explanation, language, and difficulty.

04

Publish

Only approved content appears on KEP with the correct label.

Content contribution

Prepare content the right way.

Every contributed question or paper needs subject, chapter, language, answer, explanation, and source. This will make the future admin panel clean.

Question checklist

  • Subject and chapter
  • Question text
  • Four answer options
  • Correct answer
  • Short explanation
  • Source and difficulty

Past paper checklist

  • Year or centre name
  • Official or collected label
  • Subject or full paper
  • Language
  • Permission status
  • Reviewer name or note
