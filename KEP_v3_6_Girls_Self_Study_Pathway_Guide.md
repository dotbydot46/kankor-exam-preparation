# KEP v3.6 — Girls Education Self-Study Pathway

This version adds a dedicated page: `girls-pathway.html`.

## Purpose

The page gives girls and private learners a safe, gentle weekly self-study route. It is designed for learners who may have missed months or years of school, have weak internet, use a shared device, or need to study quietly at home.

## Features

- Girls-focused hero section with the live education counter
- Neutral/safe mode button for shared devices
- Private pathway builder
- Foundation, Science, Math, Language, and Kankor Bridge focus paths
- Study-time selector
- Learning-access selector: phone only, shared device, low-data, printable
- Pashto, Dari, and English page text
- Printable weekly plan
- Local-only storage in the browser

## Privacy rule

The page must never ask for Tazkira, real name, gender proof, family details, school details, or anything sensitive. A nickname is enough.
