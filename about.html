About KEP

A learning platform for Kankor preparation, built dot by dot.

KEP helps Afghan students practise, review, plan, and prepare with more confidence — especially students who need simple online access and private self-study support.

Start Learning Girls Education

KEP promise

Simple learning. Safe content review. Honest guidance. No false promises and no claim of official exam results.

KEP is an educational support project, not an official examination authority.
Mission

What KEP is trying to solve

Many students need structure, resources, practice questions, and confidence. KEP brings these pieces together in one place.

1

Practice

Students answer approved questions and learn from explanations.

2

Progress

Students track scores, weak subjects, attempts, and next actions.

3

Support

Girls and private learners can follow a safer self-study pathway.

Trust

How KEP protects quality

  • Questions are reviewed before publishing.
  • AI suggestions are not published automatically.
  • Mock results are learning estimates, not official results.
  • Content sources and review status are tracked.
Future

Where KEP is going

KEP can grow into a stronger mobile app with more verified content, volunteer reviewers, safe AI support, and multilingual resources.
