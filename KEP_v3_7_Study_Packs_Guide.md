# KEP v3.7 — Books & Self-Study Pack System

This version adds `study-packs.html`, a printable and low-data self-study pack system.

## What it does

- Adds a new **Study Packs** page to the main navigation.
- Organizes starter packs for girls private study, foundation recovery, Kankor bridge, Math, Science, and Languages.
- Adds a weekly pack builder that creates a printable 7-day study plan.
- Saves the generated pack locally in the browser.
- Links the pack system to the Library, Practice MCQs, Girls Self-Study Pathway, and Submit Content pages.
- Updates the homepage and library page with Study Pack sections.

## Why this matters

Some students cannot stay online all day. Some use shared phones, weak internet, or need private learning. Study packs let KEP deliver content in a simple structure:

**Read a short note → practise MCQs → review mistakes → follow a weekly checklist.**

## Future version

Later, real approved books, notes, and PDFs from Google Sheets or Supabase can be connected to the pack cards. Volunteers can submit pack ideas or resources through the existing Submit Content form.
