# KEP Volunteer Outreach Messages

## Message to a Kankor centre or teacher

Assalamu Alaikum,

I hope you are well. I am working on **KEP — Kankor Exam Preparation**, a free online platform to help Afghan students prepare for Kankor through books, past papers, MCQs, mock exams, and guidance.

The aim is to support students who cannot afford courses, cannot travel safely, or need to study from home. We are looking for teachers, strong students, and volunteers who can help by reviewing questions, sharing practice materials with permission, or advising us on subject content.

Nothing will be published without review and source checking. I would be grateful if you could support, advise, or connect me with someone who may help.

## Message to students

Assalamu Alaikum,

I am building **KEP — Kankor Exam Preparation**, a free learning platform for Afghan students. You can help by testing the website, reporting wrong answers, sharing useful past papers, or joining as a volunteer.

The goal is simple: help students prepare step by step, especially those who cannot attend courses easily.
