Safe result guidance

What KEP can and cannot say after a mock exam

KEP can show

Score, percentage, time used, subject breakdown, wrong answers, weak areas, and recommended next study tasks.

KEP can estimate

Readiness level for high-competition fields such as Medicine, Engineering, Computer Science, Law, and Education.

KEP cannot promise

Official admission, exact university placement, passing/failing a field, or future Kankor result. Those belong to official authorities.

KEP helps Afghan students prepare for Kankor through learning resources, practice, mock exams, and guidance.
