Why packs?

Some students need learning they can save, print, share, or use quietly.

Instead of forcing every learner to stay online, KEP can deliver weekly PDFs, checklists, short notes, and practice tasks that work from home, village, hostel, or shared devices.

Resource governance

Every pack must be useful and trusted.

Books and notes should not be uploaded randomly. KEP should label source, language, permission, review status, and target learner.

1

Collect

Volunteer submits a book, note, formula sheet, or practice pack.

2

Check source

Mark official, teacher-created, centre-shared, or collected practice material.

3

Review

Teacher or strong student checks quality, language, and usefulness.

4

Publish

Approved packs become available in the Library, Dashboard, and Girls Pathway.
