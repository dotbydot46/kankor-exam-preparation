# KEP v2.9 — Content Review Dashboard Guide

This version adds a review layer between content submission and publishing.

## Review pipeline

1. **Collected** — Content arrives from Google Forms or a volunteer.
2. **Source Check** — The source is labelled honestly: official, textbook-based, centre practice, teacher-shared, collected practice, or unverified.
3. **Academic Review** — A teacher/reviewer checks the answer, explanation, difficulty, subject, and chapter.
4. **Translation Review** — Pashto, Dari, and English clarity are checked.
5. **Ready to Publish** — The item is approved but not yet live.
6. **Published** — The item can appear in KEP quizzes, library, or guidance pages.
7. **Rejected** — The item is inaccurate, unclear, unsafe, copyrighted without permission, or not useful.

## Important rule

Do not call a paper **official** unless the source is confirmed. Use labels such as **centre practice**, **teacher-shared**, **collected practice**, or **unverified** when needed.
