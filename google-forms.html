How to use

Share carefully. Review everything.

Use forms for collection. Use the master Sheet for checking, review, translation status, and publishing decisions.

01

Share form

Send the correct form to the right person: MCQs, papers, books, corrections, or volunteer application.

02

Check source

Label content as official, textbook-based, centre paper, teacher-shared, collected, or unverified.

03

Review

Check answers, explanations, translations, copyright status, and usefulness for students.

04

Publish

Only approved and clearly labelled content should go into KEP practice, library, or past papers.
