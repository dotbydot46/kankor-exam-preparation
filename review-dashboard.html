Question checklist

Before a question is approved

  • Subject and chapter are correct
  • Question is clear and not copied blindly
  • All four options are meaningful
  • Correct answer is verified
  • Explanation teaches the student
  • Language is natural in Pashto/Dari/English
  • Source is labelled honestly
Paper/book checklist

Before a PDF is published

  • Source or owner is known
  • Official vs centre vs collected label is clear
  • Permission/copyright status is recorded
  • File is readable and safe
  • Subject, year, language, and grade are correct
  • Reviewer name or note is saved
  • Students are not misled about authenticity
